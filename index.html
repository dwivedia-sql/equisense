Inverse Sonification

Hum or sing a curve. EquiSense listens to the pitch contour, fits the closest matching equation, and sends it straight into the navigator.

Camera Math Capture

Point the camera at one printed equation at a time. EquiSense captures the frame, runs OCR, rebuilds the math structure, and loads it into the navigator.

No camera? Upload a math image instead

Equation Navigator

Paste a LaTeX equation below and use the keyboard or buttons to move through its structure. Audio cues use spatial audio so numerators, denominators, and powers land in different places.

Try a simple fraction or the quadratic formula if you want a ready-made test.

Use ↑ to go up, ↓ to go deeper, ←/→ to move sideways, Space to speak the current node, and R to read the whole equation.

Graph Sonifier

Upload a two-column CSV (x, y) and listen to the data as audio. EquiSense announces a short description first, then plays the trend so you know what to expect.
